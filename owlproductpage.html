Show your products in tabs anywhere in your store showing great amount of information in a small space.
The mod has a lot of easy configuration options and thanks to the separation between tabs and the module in the settings you can create a tab (defining products to be displayed inside and the title) and display it on many modules as you want across your store.

Features:
  • Multilanguage.
  • Simple configuration.
  • Definition of separate tabs.
  • Create as many modules as you want.
  • 3 different types of product selection
Product Selection:
  • List specific products (Choosen with autocomplet)
  • By group (Bestseller, latest, Popular or Special)
  • Custom Query (Choose category, manufacturer and order...)
